Privacy Policy

Memori ("the App") respects your privacy.

Information Collection

The App does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or phone number.

An anonymous user identifier is generated and used solely to:

  • enable cloud synchronization,
  • track subscription status,
  • manage AI feature usage limits.

Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services including:

  • Firebase (data storage and cloud functions)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • Google Cloud AI services (AI-generated explanations and pronunciation)

These services may process anonymized usage data as required to provide their functionality.

Data Usage

All collected data is used solely to provide app functionality and is not used for advertising or tracking purposes.
